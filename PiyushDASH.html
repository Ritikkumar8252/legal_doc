AI Summary

This contract is between NexGen Solutions and Arjun Mehta for a freelance full-stack SaaS platform. Payment is INR 85,000 (20% upfront, 80% on delivery). The freelancer must deliver within 30 days with daily progress reports. The client can terminate at any time without notice. Key concerns include unlimited revisions, IP ownership transfer, and one-sided termination clauses — the contract significantly favors the client.
