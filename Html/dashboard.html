This agreement grants Company X (Client) rights to use Y's SaaS platform for 12 months starting January 2025. The client pays ₹5,000/month, billed on the 1st, with a 7-day grace period. Either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice; early termination by the client incurs a 3-month penalty fee. Y's liability is capped at 3 months of fees paid.
